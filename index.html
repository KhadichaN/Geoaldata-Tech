Road Crack Detection

  • Automate Road Crack Detection for DOTs and Public Works

Road Asset Extraction from Oriented Imagery

  • Water meters, road signs, trash bins, pavement markings, etc.
  • Geotagging of assets
  • Results as a feature layer consumable in ArcGIS Pro or ArcGIS Enterprise

Asset Extraction from Lidar

  • Trees, Signs, Traffic Lights, Guard Rails, etc.
  • Results as a Feature Layer - consumable in ArcGIS Pro or ArcGIS Enterprise
  • Automate - saves analysts' time to focus on analysis and other projects
